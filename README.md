## 🌌 THE JOURNEY

Five sealed doors stand across a ruined land. They are anchors of memory, fragmented by time and emotion. Beyond each door waits the Ever-After—a realm of untamed chaos. 

Your journey will never be the same twice. With every attempt to close the gates, reality shifts and twists the landscape into new forms. You must step through each door, confront the memory hidden within, and lock it safely away before the chaos bleeds through.

## 🔀 THE SHIFTING REALITY

The world reshapes itself whenever you begin. You will face trials drawn from a vast pool of forgotten moments, ensuring that no two journeys ever unfold the same way. 

As you travel, hidden atmospheres may silently overtake your path, bending the world to their will:
- 🌫️ **Memory:** A lingering sadness; the world slows and blurs, lost in thought.
- 🪞 **Illusion:** The environment distorts, testing your perception of what is real.
- 🏃 **Chase:** Unrelenting pressure; adrenaline pushes you forward without rest.
- 🌸 **Calm:** A fleeting moment of peace, a breath of quiet before the final seal.

## 🚪 WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

The doors do not ask for strength—they ask for understanding. To close them, you must immerse yourself in what was lost:

> **Solve broken memories** that have shattered across the ruins.  
> **Survive collapsing worlds** as the ground gives way beneath you.  
> **Follow forgotten rhythms** to unlock the truth.  
> **Choose between realities** when illusions try to deceive your eyes.  
> **Unlock sealed doors of emotion** to restore balance to the land.

## 🌅 THE FINAL DOOR

The fifth door is not merely an obstacle—it is a conclusion. It is the culmination of every memory, every trial, and every step taken through the ruins. 

To complete it is to find closure. The locked memories are laid to rest. The chaotic forces of the Ever-After recede, and the ruined landscape is bathed in the warm, quiet light of a new dawn. 

*You have returned what was lost.*
